01 — The eBay Seller Workflow

How Glassio Works with eBay

eBay provides sellers with the ability to export their listing data as CSV files directly from Seller Hub. Glassio is built to work seamlessly with this workflow — import your eBay CSV reports into Glassio and immediately gain a clear, organized view of your entire inventory.

  • Export from eBay Seller Hub — Download your active listings, sold items, or inventory reports as CSV from eBay's Seller Hub or My eBay dashboard
  • Import into Glassio — Drag and drop or import your CSV file into Glassio in seconds
  • Manage centrally — Edit quantities, add notes, track costs, and organize your full eBay inventory catalog from one Mac-native workspace
  • Export back to eBay — When ready, export updated inventory data as CSV to bulk-upload changes back to eBay Seller Hub

No API key required. Glassio works entirely through CSV import and export — the same format eBay already supports. Your data stays on your Mac, never on a third-party server.

02 — What You Can Track

Full eBay Inventory Control on Mac

As an eBay seller, keeping accurate inventory records is critical to preventing overselling, managing restocks, and understanding your business performance. Glassio gives you the tools to stay on top of every listing:

  • Track stock quantities per SKU across your entire eBay catalog
  • Catalog items by category, condition, price, cost, and location
  • Monitor which items are running low and need restocking
  • Record purchase costs, sale prices, and margins for profitability tracking
  • Manage inventory across multiple storage locations or warehouses
  • Keep notes and custom fields per item for condition grading or provenance
  • Track high-value items like collectibles, antiques, or banknotes with precision
03 — Why Local-First is Better for eBay Sellers

Your Inventory Data, Your Control

Most inventory tools for eBay sellers require cloud accounts, monthly subscriptions, and give third-party platforms access to your sensitive business data. Glassio takes a different approach:

  • Local-first storage — All your eBay inventory data is saved on your Mac, not on external servers
  • No API key exposure — You don't connect Glassio directly to your eBay account, eliminating the risk of API key leaks or unauthorized access
  • Works offline — Manage your inventory anywhere, even without an internet connection
  • No subscription lock-in — Pay once (or download free), and your inventory tool is yours
  • Data portability — Export your full catalog at any time as CSV, giving you complete freedom to move or migrate your data

Perfect for high-volume eBay sellers, collectible resellers, and power sellers who need a reliable, private, and fast inventory management tool on their Mac.
