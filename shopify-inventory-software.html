01 — The Shopify Seller Workflow

How Glassio Works with Shopify

Shopify makes it easy to export your product catalog and inventory data as CSV files directly from the admin panel. Glassio is built to work with these standard exports — giving Shopify merchants a powerful Mac-native tool to manage inventory outside of the Shopify interface.

  • Export from Shopify Admin — Go to Products in your Shopify admin and export your catalog as a CSV file (Shopify supports full product and inventory CSV exports natively)
  • Import into Glassio — Import your Shopify CSV into Glassio and instantly have your full product catalog organized by SKU, variant, collection, and quantity
  • Manage and update — Edit stock levels, add cost data, track low inventory, and organize products across categories from your Mac
  • Re-import to Shopify — Export your updated inventory from Glassio and re-import it into Shopify via the Products CSV import feature to keep your store in sync

No Shopify app installation required. Glassio uses Shopify's built-in CSV export format. No API connection, no app permissions, no monthly app fees — just clean CSV-based inventory management on your Mac.

02 — What You Can Track

Complete Shopify Product Inventory on Mac

Managing a Shopify store means handling products with multiple variants, collections, and inventory locations. Glassio gives Shopify merchants a structured inventory system to stay on top of it all:

  • Track stock quantities per SKU and product variant (size, color, material)
  • Organize products by collection, vendor, product type, and tags
  • Monitor which variants are low in stock and need restocking
  • Record unit cost (COGS) and compare against Shopify sale prices for margin analysis
  • Track inventory across multiple warehouse or storage locations
  • Add internal notes per product for supplier info, reorder quantities, or condition
  • Manage seasonal or limited-edition product launches with a clear inventory picture
