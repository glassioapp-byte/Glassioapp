01 — The Amazon Seller Workflow

How Glassio Works with Amazon Seller Central

Amazon Seller Central provides sellers with detailed inventory and sales reports that can be exported as CSV files. Glassio is designed to work directly with these reports — bringing your Amazon product data into a clean, powerful Mac-native workspace.

  • Export from Seller Central — Download your inventory report, FBA inventory report, or restock report from Amazon Seller Central as CSV
  • Import into Glassio — Load your Amazon CSV report into Glassio and instantly view your full inventory catalog organized by ASIN, SKU, and quantity
  • Track and manage — Monitor stock levels, set restock thresholds, log costs, and categorize products across FBA and FBM fulfillment methods
  • Export for updates — Generate updated inventory or pricing CSV files ready for bulk upload back to Seller Central

No Seller Central API connection required. Glassio uses the standard CSV workflow Amazon already provides. Your sensitive seller data — margins, costs, pricing strategy — stays on your Mac.

02 — What You Can Track

Complete Amazon Inventory Management on Mac

Amazon sellers need to track more than just quantity. Glassio gives you a structured, flexible inventory system for everything that matters to your Amazon business:

  • Track available stock and inbound FBA quantities per ASIN and SKU
  • Monitor stranded inventory and flag items needing attention
  • Record COGS (cost of goods sold) per unit for profitability analysis
  • Track supplier information, lead times, and reorder points
  • Manage multiple product variations (size, color, bundle) in a single catalog view
  • Add custom notes per product — useful for restricted item compliance or condition tracking
  • Monitor inventory age and storage fee risks for FBA items
