What Is Glassio

Inventory Clarity,
Delivered Natively on Mac

Glassio is a Mac desktop application built to give businesses full control over their inventory, stock levels, product data, and operational workflows — all within one clean, fast, and beautifully designed interface.

Import thousands of rows via CSV, track stock across multiple locations, monitor availability in real time, and export organized reports — without touching a spreadsheet or managing another web dashboard.

Built exclusively for macOS, Glassio runs as a true native desktop app: fast, reliable, offline-capable, and designed to feel at home on your Mac.

Inventory Tracking CSV Import / Export Multi-Location Product Management macOS Native Real-Time Stock
📋

The Problem

Businesses waste hours maintaining inventory across disconnected spreadsheets, losing track of stock levels, making costly errors in manual CSV files, and lacking a clear operational picture.

✦

The Glassio Difference

One Mac-native desktop workspace that centralizes your inventory data, automates CSV workflows, and gives you a real-time visual picture of your entire operation.

🎯

Who It's Built For

Product businesses, wholesalers, retailers, fulfilment operations, and any team that manages stock and needs reliable, clear, desktop-grade inventory tools on macOS.

How It Works

Operational in Minutes

Glassio is designed for speed of setup. No complex configuration, no IT required — get your inventory into the system and under control from day one.

1

Download & Install on Mac

Get Glassio from the Mac App Store or direct download. It installs like any native Mac app — no configuration scripts, no web setup, no browser tabs.

2

Import Your Inventory via CSV

Bring your existing product data in instantly with CSV import. Glassio maps your columns, validates your data, and populates your full inventory in seconds.

3

Track, Manage & Export

Monitor stock levels, update quantities, assign locations, manage product images, and export clean reports — all from one desktop workspace.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you download.

Glassio is a Mac desktop application for inventory management, product tracking, and operational data workflows. It lets you import and manage your product catalog, track stock levels across locations, visualize data with charts, and export clean CSV reports — all from a native Mac interface.
Glassio is currently a Mac desktop application only — built exclusively for macOS. It requires macOS 14 Sonoma or later. Windows and iPad versions are not currently available, as Glassio is designed to be a Mac-native experience from the ground up.
Click "Insert CSV" in any inventory view and select your file. Glassio will detect your column headers and let you map them to the correct fields — SKU, name, location, stock quantity, cost, and more. Validation runs automatically before any data is written, so you can catch errors before they affect your catalog.
No. All of your inventory data is stored locally on your Mac. Glassio does not require an internet connection and does not upload your data to any external server. You own your data completely, with no cloud lock-in or third-party access.
Glassio is built to handle catalogs of any size — from a small business with dozens of SKUs to operations managing hundreds of thousands of product records. The local database architecture ensures performance stays fast regardless of catalog size.
Real-time tracking in Glassio means your stock levels, availability statuses, and location data update instantly as you make changes — no refresh required. The dashboard and inventory views always reflect the current state of your data as you edit, import, or reconcile records.
Glassio is free to download with full access to core features. A one-time upgrade is available for businesses that need advanced features such as expanded location tracking, full CSV automation workflows, and priority support. There are no monthly fees or recurring subscription charges.
